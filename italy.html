How to Get There

Direct flights from Dubai (DXB) or Abu Dhabi (AUH) to Leonardo da Vinci International Airport (FCO) take around 6 hours. Emirates, Etihad, and ITA Airways operate direct flights. From the airport, you can take the Leonardo Express train (32 minutes to Termini Station), taxis, or airport shuttles to the city center.


Best Time to Visit

Spring (April–June) and autumn (September–November) offer pleasant temperatures and fewer tourists. Summer can be very hot and crowded.


Things to See

The Colosseum, an ancient gladiator arena; the Pantheon, an architectural marvel; the Trevi Fountain, where you can toss a coin for good luck; the Vatican City, home to St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel with Michelangelo’s frescoes; and the Roman Forum, once the center of Roman civilization.
