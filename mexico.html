How to Get There

Flights from Dubai (DXB) to Mexico City International Airport (MEX) require at least one layover, often in the U.S. or Europe, and take around 20–24 hours depending on the route. Major airlines include Emirates, Air France, and Turkish Airlines. Upon arrival, take authorized airport taxis, Uber, or the Metrobus to reach the city center.


Best Time to Visit

March–May for warm but comfortable weather. The winter months (December–February) are also good for sightseeing. Avoid the rainy season (June–September).


Things to See

The Teotihuacán Pyramids, an ancient Mesoamerican city; Chapultepec Castle, offering amazing views and history; the Frida Kahlo Museum, showcasing the life and art of Mexico’s famous painter; Zócalo Square, the historic heart of Mexico City; and Xochimilco, where you can take a colorful boat ride along traditional canals.
