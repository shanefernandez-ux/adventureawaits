How to Get There

Direct flights from Dubai (DXB) to Narita International Airport (NRT) or Haneda Airport (HND) take around 9–10 hours. Airlines like Emirates and Japan Airlines operate direct routes. From the airport, take the Narita Express train (1 hour to Tokyo Station) or Haneda Monorail (20 minutes to central Tokyo).


Best Time to Visit

Spring (March–May) for cherry blossoms and autumn (September–November) for colorful foliage. Summer can be humid, and winter is cold but still enjoyable.


Things to See

Shibuya Crossing, one of the world’s busiest pedestrian crossings; Tokyo Tower, offering panoramic city views; Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo’s oldest temple; Akihabara, a paradise for anime and tech lovers; and a day trip to Mount Fuji for stunning scenery.
